Design Thinking & Creativity Lab

Project Case Study: Jaymani Krishi Sewa

Team: Manish Pandey & Jayant Raj | Mentor: Ms. Sheenam Naaz

1. Empathy Mapping (Understanding Farmers)

Humne rural markets ka visit kiya aur paya ki kisanon ka 30% samay aur fuel sirf stock ki jankari lene mein barbaad ho jata hai.

2. 5W & 1H Analysis

Who? Small-scale & marginal farmers.
What? Information gap in supply and pricing.
Where? Rural agricultural markets.
When? Peak agricultural/sowing seasons.
Why? Prevents financial loss and crop failure.
How? Through digital marketplace & SMS.

3. Problem Statement

"Farmers lack advance information on stock availability and transparent pricing, leading to significant time and productivity loss."

4. Prototype Development Steps

1
Ideation: Multi-vendor model to include local retailers like Pandey Traders.
2
Features: SMS-based access for low internet areas.
3
Business Model: Commission-based revenue for sustainability.
